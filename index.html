How It Works

Simple, Fast, and Built on Trust

Sending a referral takes seconds. Collecting your finder's fee is automatic.

1

Send a Referral

Got a client who needs work you don't do? Send a referral to someone in your network with the client's info and job details.

2

Partner Accepts

Your partner reviews the referral and accepts it. If they're not the right fit, they can decline and you can send it to someone else.

3

You Get Paid

Once your partner accepts, your referral fee is paid automatically. No chasing payments, no awkward conversations.
