Frequently Asked Questions

Download the ReferVo app from the App Store or Google Play. Open the app and tap "Create Account." Enter your email, create a password, and fill in your basic business information. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

ReferVo is designed for any professional or business that receives leads or referrals. This includes contractors, real estate agents, consultants, agencies, freelancers, accountants, lawyers, and many more.

From your dashboard, tap "Send Lead" and select the partner you want to refer to. Enter the lead's information (name, contact details, and any relevant notes). Your partner will receive a notification with all the details.

When you send a lead that converts into a paying customer, your partner marks the lead as "converted" and processes the referral payment through ReferVo. Funds are deposited to your connected bank account within 2-3 business days.

ReferVo charges a small transaction fee (currently 5%) on referral payments processed through our platform. There are no monthly fees, no signup costs, and no charges for sending or receiving leads.
