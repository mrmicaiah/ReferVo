Welcome to ReferVo! Whether you're a contractor looking to monetize your network or a business owner wanting to receive quality referrals, this guide will walk you through everything you need to get started.

## Step 1: Download and Sign Up

First things first—grab the app from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6759116716). Android is coming soon, so sign up for our waitlist if you're on Android.

When you sign up, you'll choose your account type:

- **Business Account** – For service providers who want to receive leads AND send referrals
- **Personal Account (Power Networker)** – For people who send referrals but don't receive them

Most service professionals choose a Business Account, but if you're someone who knows a lot of people and wants to earn from those connections without running a service business, the Personal Account is perfect.

## Step 2: Complete Your Profile

Your profile is your first impression. Here's what to include:

### Business Name
Use the name your clients know you by. This appears when you send referrals and helps partners recognize you.

### Industry & Services
Be specific. Instead of just "Construction," list your actual services like "Kitchen Remodeling, Bathroom Renovation, General Contracting." This helps partners know exactly what types of leads to send you.

### Service Area
Set your geographic coverage so you only receive leads you can actually take.

### Connection Fee
This is what you charge for **receiving** a referral. It's the fee you pay to the person who sends you a lead. Industry standard ranges from 3-10% of the job value, or a flat fee for smaller jobs.

## Step 3: Connect with Partners

ReferVo is built on trusted relationships. You won't see random strangers—only people you've connected with.

### How to Add Connections

1. **Search by name or email** – If they're already on ReferVo, send a connection request
2. **Invite via link** – Share your unique invite link with people who aren't on the platform yet
3. **Accept incoming requests** – Check your pending requests for people trying to connect with you

### Who Should You Connect With?

Think about two things:

1. **Who sends you work?** – Real estate agents, property managers, other contractors in complementary trades
2. **Who can you send work to?** – Trusted service providers you'd confidently recommend to your own clients

## Step 4: Set Up Payments

To receive referral fee payouts, you'll need to connect your Stripe account. This takes about 2 minutes:

1. Go to **Settings > Payments**
2. Tap **Connect Stripe Account**
3. Follow the prompts to enter your bank details

ReferVo uses Stripe's secure Express accounts, so your financial information is never stored on our servers.

## Step 5: Send Your First Referral

Now for the fun part—actually using the app. Here's how to send a lead:

1. Tap the **Send Lead** button
2. **Choose a partner** – Select from your connections
3. **Add lead details** – Name, phone, what they need
4. **Set your referral fee** – Choose a percentage or flat amount
5. **Send it** – Your partner gets a notification instantly

### What Happens Next?

Your partner will see the lead and can:
- **Accept** – They're taking the job and agree to your fee
- **Decline** – Not a fit for them right now

Once they accept and close the deal, they mark it complete, and your referral fee is processed automatically.

## Tips for Success

### Be Responsive
When you receive a lead, respond quickly. Leads go cold fast, and your partners are trusting you with their client relationships.

### Send Quality, Not Quantity
Don't spam your partners with every inquiry. Send leads that are actually a good fit for their services. Quality referrals lead to repeat partnerships.

### Follow Up
Check in on your sent leads. The app shows you the status, but a quick message to your partner can help ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

### Build Genuine Relationships
ReferVo works best when built on real trust. Meet your connections in person when possible. The stronger your relationships, the more referrals will flow both ways.

## Common Questions

**How much does ReferVo cost?**
ReferVo is free to download and use. We take a small platform fee from completed referral transactions.

**What if a deal doesn't close?**
No closed deal = no fees. You only pay referral fees on successful jobs.

**Can I set different fees for different partners?**
Yes! You set the fee each time you send a lead, so you can customize based on the job and relationship.
